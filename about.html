About CoffeeBox

Born from Coffeeberry, CoffeeBox pioneered KwaZulu-Natal’s first drive-thru coffee at Athlone Circle — premium coffee, fast service, and community at the heart of everything.

Try our signature coffees

Our Story

What began as Coffeeberry at Cascades Mall grew into a new idea: quality coffee for people on the move. CoffeeBox was created for busy commuters and local families alike — a place where speed and craftsmanship meet.

Mission

Deliver premium, freshly brewed coffee quickly while supporting Pietermaritzburg’s community through partnerships and responsible practices.

Vision

To become South Africa’s most loved drive-thru coffee brand — known for consistent quality and friendly service.

Values

  • Quality — premium beans, roasted for consistency.
  • Speed — drive-thru efficiency without sacrificing flavour.
  • Community — local partnerships and sponsorships.
  • Care — trained baristas and excellent customer service.
